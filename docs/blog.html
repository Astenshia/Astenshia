Computer projects

LABYRINTH

My current game project : I am trying to make a 3D randomly generated maze. A protagonist will discover a lot of secrets and maybe understand why he ended up inside...

Realized in C# with openGL libraries.

Collaboration with Etikun, Louvtt.

2022

BATTLESHIP

The classic game of battleship, where you have to find the location of the opponent's ships to sink them. The first one to lose all his ships is the loser!

Realized in JAVA.

Collaboration with Louvtt.

October 2022

GMTK JAM 2022 - UNDICISIOUS

Summer 2022 game jam theme: roll the dice! With the guys, we decided to make a multidimensional dice game: you play up to 3 games at the same time, and the goal is to make a maximum of tricks, to score a maximum of points.

Realized with Godot.

Collaboration with Etikun, Louvtt, Aster.

July 2022

PANDEMY SIMULATOR

A simulation of different types of pandemics, based on real scientific models. You can vary different parameters depending on the model you choose, and see the population evolve!

Realized in HTML, CSS, JavaScript.

Collaboration with Louvtt.

June 2022

Cinematographic production

LE PLOC

Une jeune fille passe une soirée seule avec son chien. Cependant, un étrange son va perturber sa nuit...

Tournage : Juillet 2022
Sortie : Novembre 2022

L'OMBRE D'UN DOUTE

Rey Ketsuna, jeune chasseuse de primes spécialisée dans l'art de l'assassinat, se rend chez M. George Angster, un trafiquant qui capture et revend des créatures en tout genre.
Parmi celles-ci, une seule correspond réellement aux attentes de la mercenaire.
Une certaine tension s'installe entre nos deux personnages, se connaissent-ils réellement ?

Tournage : Août 2020
Sortie : Juin 2021
